How to explore
Your complete guide
1
Position your phone
Hold your phone at arm's length, pointed at the Hermès window display. Keep it steady while the AR engine scans.
2
Watch the birds appear
Once the window is detected, a flock of animated birds will emerge — layered into the real scene through your camera.
3
Listen to the window
Audio plays and shifts with time of day. At dawn, hear the atelier. At dusk, perhaps distant horses on cobblestone.
4
Take something with you
Visit the Story page to download wallpapers as keepsakes — a fragment of the window, yours to keep long after the season ends.
